About ☆⌒ Algorithmic Artscape ｡･:*:･ﾟ’★

Algorithmic Artscape: a Platform for Exploring Distributed Community Creativity is a project by Celeste Betancur; and Joana Chicau; developed during Tekhne online residency (2024) in collaboration with Q-O2 workspace and Trafo gallery.

Algorithmic Artscape integrates different tools to compose audio and visuals. Find a list to further documentation below:

  • Hydra is an analog-synth-like coding environment for real-time visuals created by Olivia Jack: Link to Hydra Book;
  • Chuck is our audio engine, we are using webchuck integration, for more info: Webchuck
  • Soundscape AI

Interested in contributing with an artwork? Get in touch!

For bug fixes or if you would like to support the development of the platform: check our GitLab repo!

Colophon: Typefaces Goozette and Goozee by Madeleine Barbaroux and Alexandra Lambert; and Gudea by Agustina Mingote .
